Our Story

From Real Trades
to Real Education

Trends & Trade was founded with a single belief: that quality trading education should be accessible to everyone. Too many traders lose money not because markets are unfair, but because they never received proper guidance.

We built this platform around the S&R Intelligent Trading System — a methodology developed through years of live trading across Stocks, Crypto, Gold, and Forex. Every concept we teach is battle-tested in real markets, not assembled from textbooks.

Today, Trends & Trade serves students across four continents, offering free courses, market analysis, and a community where traders at every level can learn, grow, and trade with confidence.
